# TreeHawk

TreeHawk builds at the intersection of **development**, **AI**, and practical **business value**.

We focus on creating digital solutions that are not only technically solid, but also useful in real operational contexts. That includes custom software, modern web platforms, AI-supported workflows, and consulting that connects technology with business decisions.

## What We Do

- Custom web development
- AI-driven solutions and automation
- Shopware development and integrations
- Laravel applications and backend platforms
- IT, AI, and business consulting

## Focus Areas

### Development

We design and build maintainable software with a strong focus on clarity, scalability, and long-term usability. Our work ranges from websites and custom applications to backend services, integrations, and internal tools.

### AI

We use AI where it creates real leverage: automation, content workflows, intelligent assistants, internal tooling, and decision support. The goal is not AI for hype, but AI that saves time, improves quality, and fits the business process.

### Shopware

We support Shopware projects with custom development, extension work, integrations, process optimization, and technical guidance for commerce environments that need to scale cleanly.

### Laravel

Laravel is one of our preferred foundations for modern application development. We use it to build robust platforms, APIs, admin systems, and business applications with clean architecture and strong developer ergonomics.

### Consulting

We combine technical execution with strategic thinking across:

- IT consulting
- AI consulting
- business consulting

That means helping teams not only choose the right tools, but also structure projects, processes, and priorities in a way that creates measurable outcomes.
